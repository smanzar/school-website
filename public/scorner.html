Co-Curricular Activities

Students also participate in different result oriented co-curricular activities to utilize their grasped knowledge and information regarding to their academic level, such as, quizes on Language, Literature, Science and Maths in addition Art and Craft making competitions enhance their creative skills towards challenges of life.
Working at projects on 3D models conducted by an authentic External Board which boosts up their skills of observations, creation and crafting to build up a graph for successful field work after completing their education.
All the participants have got a valuable importance in this scenario and therefore, been awarded with appreciation certificates and shields for being the efficient and effective part of the process.

Extra Curricular Activities

SoE does not only fulfil the academic demands of the students but also very keenly gives its students an opportunity to co-relate their education to the external environment also. We arrange several Excursion trips, Annual evening, Yearly Award Function, Sports Day, Eid Parties, in order to elevate a sense of social collaboration and ethical values among the students.
