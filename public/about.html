Our Mission

Tungsten school of excellence strives to provide an environment conducive to your child’s academic, intellectual ,physical, moral and social growth.
Students are motivated to express themselves freely. They are helped by the experienced and well qualified teachers, who facilitate the research, explain concepts and harmonize meaningful discussions.

Our Vision

Our vision is to prepare students for future for the real world and provide learning opportunities that promote personal values, community norms and moral, critical thinking and problem solving skills






ABOUT US

TSE was established on 1st August`1999 with an aim to promote and develop knowledge in a unique and extra ordinary environment.The institution offers standards from Reception, Junior-I, Junior-II to O`levels & Matric and is affiliated with Cambridge University U.K. and Aga Khan Examination Board.
