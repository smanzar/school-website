Academic Envirnment

Our academic environment has been set on the basis of catering the demands of modern and international system of education, where children are provided with the methodology of demonstrative and contrast studies. They are given a complete plan of using their own knowledge, observation and conclusion to accomplish their academic projects.

ABOUT US

TSE was established on 1st August`1999 with an aim to promote and develop knowledge in a unique and extra ordinary environment.The institution offers standards from Reception, Junior-I, Junior-II to O`levels & Matric and is affiliated with Cambridge University U.K. and Aga Khan Examination Board.
